We Are Triangle

A fraternity of engineers, architects, and scientists that develops balanced men who cultivate high moral character, foster lifelong friendships, and live their lives with integrity.

Our organization is dedicated to helping men with interests in these areas realize their full potential in college and develop into well-rounded, academically driven men who are involved on campus.

About Us

We own two houses in the Dinkytown area, located at the intersection of 12th Ave SE and 6th St SE.

Information about our National Organization can be found at triangle.org.

Join Triangle

We are looking for undergraduate men that want to be academically successful and that lead by example. If you are interested in joining Triangle Fraternity please either email our VP of Recruitment at triangle@umn.edu or join us for one of our upcoming rush events.
